About

My name is Parker Olvera. I was born in Turlock, California and I’ve lived here my whole life.

When it comes to hobbies, creating art is my favorite thing to do. Over the course of my years in high school, I became more and more interested in all types of digital art. Two years ago, I got a DSLR camera for my birthday and ever since then, I have been obsessed with photography and photo editing. My senior year of high school, I worked on creating a photography portfolio for one of my classes and I’ve created some of my favorite works during that time. My passion for photography led to developing a love for digital art and eventually graphic design which is what I am majoring in at SJSU.

So that’s basically me. I’m a nerdy artist from the central valley who knows about camping. I can’t wait to learn at SJSU and see what life has in store for me.
